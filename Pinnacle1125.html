Pinnacle is designed for players who want to take their game to the highest levels of college lacrosse. Pinnacle directors curate an environment that will prepare players for the speed, intensity and workload they will experience at the college level. In addition, each player will have mentorship through the recruiting process with the goal of finding the right fit for each player academically, athletically and socially.The foundation of the Pinnacle program is our week-long, summer team trip where the players compete in two top tier recruiting events. These Pinnacle trips include campus visits, recruiting mentorship, and tours of some of our nation's most historic sites.
